FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

What does gas hob deep cleaning include?
+

Deep cleaning covers full disassembly of burner caps, nozzles, drip trays, and control knobs. We use professional-grade degreasers to remove all grease and food residue, then reassemble and test performance.

How often should I service my gas stove or hob?
+

We recommend a professional service every 6–12 months depending on usage. Regular servicing prevents gas leaks, improves flame quality, and extends the life of your appliance.

Do you use genuine spare parts?
+

Yes! We stock OEM-compatible and original spare parts for all major brands including Elica, Faber, Prestige, Pigeon, Kaff, and more. You can verify the parts used during the visit.

Is chimney PCB repair possible or do I need a new chimney?
+

In most cases, PCB repair is possible and much more economical than buying a new chimney. Our technicians diagnose the exact fault on the circuit board and repair or replace only the affected component.

How quickly can you provide service?
+

We offer same-day service for most areas in Hyderabad. For urgent gas leakage issues, we prioritize visits. Simply call or WhatsApp us and we will schedule at your earliest convenience.

What warranty do you offer on repairs?
+

All repairs come with a service warranty. The duration depends on the type of repair and parts used. Our technician will explain the warranty terms clearly before starting work.

Is LPG copper pipe installation safe at home?
+

Absolutely, when done by certified professionals. We use ISI-marked copper pipes and fittings, perform pressure testing, and verify zero leakage before completing the installation.
